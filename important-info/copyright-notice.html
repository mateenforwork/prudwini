To do so, you must provide our Copyright Agent with the following:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of a person permitted to act on behalf of the copyright owner;
  2. Identification of the material that you believe has been improperly copied, published or displayed. If you believe there is more than one instance of such improper use of materials at our online website, include a list;
  3. Identification, by location or link, of the material or activity that you believe infringed on your copyright and that is to be removed, or access to which is to be disabled;
  4. Information that enables HCSC to contact you, such as an address, telephone number and email address;
