Professional Development

 

At HCSC, each employee owns a vital stake in building an even stronger, even smarter and more efficient organization. We are committed to discovering new ideas, finding a better way, and asking the right questions. Ongoing learning and development opportunities help foster this kind of creative thought.

We seek talented individuals passionate about the work they do and the impact they seek to have as they progress in their careers. If that's you, here's how HCSC can help you realize your fullest career potential.

Education Assistance

We believe in your continued growth and education. HCSC provides up to $5,250 (tax free) annually for tuition toward a degree, or $2,000 toward certificates or professional licenses.

Learning Programs

Onboarding

Prior to your first day at HCSC, you'll receive helpful information to prepare you for your new job through our online onboarding tool, Red Carpet. HCSC offers 90-day onboarding programs for all employees, including specific focus areas for new leaders.

Professional Development

HCSC encourages development at all levels, allowing employees to build and enhance skills through a variety of programs and opportunities. We empower employees to think through their individual career goals and needs and understand individual opportunities for development using a variety of tools and programs.

In addition to our in-house development options, HCSC partners with select universities in our operating states to support continuing education programs that are relevant and add value to our employees as they strive to grow professionally.

Leadership Development

We offer companywide programs that provide all leaders the opportunity to strengthen their management skills and develop leadership behaviors. Transitioning to new leadership roles creates new opportunities for development. We support those transitions by providing development programs aligned to the specific skill requirements of each new career level. Our LEAD profile is the foundation for all our leadership training programs:

  • Live our Purpose
  • Engage with People
  • Adapt for the Future
  • Drive Results
