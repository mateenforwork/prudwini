Beyond the Numbers

As a member of the accounting and finance areas of Health Care Service Corporation (HCSC), you'll build upon our history of exceptional financial strength to apply new ways of thinking and create meaningful advancements that benefit our members. HCSC is not a publicly traded company and does not have any investors—we are customer-owned. Our "investors" are our policy holders; we answer to them and will always make their best interests our top priority.

At HCSC, you'll be challenged to conceive of better ways, with the encouragement to explore impacts beyond your immediate task. Because at HCSC, we're reimagining health care and looking to set new standards, for our customers, our industry and ourselves. Consider a career with HCSC and discover more than you thought possible.
