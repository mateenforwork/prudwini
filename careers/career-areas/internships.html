Real Experience for the Real World

HCSC recruits promising talent at colleges and universities throughout the country. In addition to our core universities and colleges, HCSC is a frequent participant in the National Black MBA Career Success Network & Career Expo, Student Veterans of America, Chicago Sky Veteran & Diversity Hiring Expo, Prospanica, and Hispanic Alliance for Career Enhancement (HACE). The ultimate goal of our internship program is converting high performing interns into full-time colleagues and building an ongoing talent pipeline.

Here's what you can anticipate in an HCSC internship:

  • Formal Intern Orientation to build business and industry acumen
  • Challenging real world hands on projects
  • Weekly networking opportunities among interns and company leaders
  • Professional development workshops and mentorship
  • Business Resource Group involvement
  • Volunteer activities
  • City-specific outing
