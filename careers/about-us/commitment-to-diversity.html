Commitment to Diversity

Our Mission

At HCSC, we value diversity and inclusion because diverse perspectives, experiences and approaches foster a stronger, smarter and more informed business. We continue to strive to create an environment where employees are valued, empowered to positively support our business objectives, and recognized for their individual contributions.

At HCSC, we are committed to maintaining an environment of Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action. To request special accommodation to submit an application, please contact us at: 866.977.7378 or email us at: HREmployeeServices@bcbstx.com.

  • Getting Involved

    HCSC has 9 Business Resource Groups and 3 Employee Networks to foster connections, support and engagement. These groups are strategically linked to our business through their work around careers, community and culture.

    • African Americans in Motion
    • Asian American Business Resource Group
    • Engaging Millennial Energy to Recognize Growth Enterprise-wide
    • In-Abled
    • Latinos for Advancement
    • Native Americans in Progress
    • Pride Alliance
    • Supporters of Military Veterans
    • Women Improving the Strength of the Enterprise
    • The Diversity Forum — seeks to foster an inclusive and culturally competent workplace in which employees share a mutual understanding and respect for each other.
    • Europa — focuses on educating, uniting, and communicating with European ethnic communities on the topics of health and wellness
    • Voices of Diversity — a music ensemble that performs concerts throughout the year

    — Paula Steiner – President and Chief Executive Officer Health Care Service Corporation
