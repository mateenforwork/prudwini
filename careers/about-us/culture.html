Purpose-Driven Performance

HCSC is an organization of purpose-driven performance. With our members at the center of all we do, we bring authenticity to our work and innovate for competitive advantage, for our members and our company. In our communities, we improve health and wellness by looking for areas of opportunity—investing time and resources with over 370 community partners across our five states. We embrace open lines of communication, inclusion in all its forms and courageous feedback. We are adaptable to market demands and are shaping the landscape of health care…together.

At its core, HCSC is about respect—for our members, our community and our organization. We express this respect by reimagining what’s possible, across all disciplines and at all levels. We are a company that welcomes great ideas, with the strength to create genuine, meaningful change.

Team members listen to, learn from, support and encourage one another. As the preferred health insurance plan of Illinois, Montana, New Mexico, Oklahoma and Texas, we work hard day-by-day to earn the trust that has been placed in us. That's what winning teams do.
