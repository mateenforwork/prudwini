Our Hiring Process

Our Application Process

At HCSC, we understand your time is important. It is our goal to make things as simple and transparent as possible around our application process so you’ll know what to expect. Here’s a step-by-step look.

Search, Explore & Apply

All of our open jobs are listed on our website. Search for jobs that fit your background and career interests. Once you see a job that interests you, click the “Submit Now�? to start your application. If you do not see a job that fits your skills or interests, register to receive email notifications as new jobs become available.

At HCSC, we are committed to maintaining an environment of Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action. To request special accommodation to submit an application, please contact us at: 866.977.7378 or email us at: HCSC_AskMyHR@ehr.com.

The Application

Once you click “Submit Now�? you will be asked to either login if you have previously created a profile or create an account by registering with your email and a password.

Once you have logged in or created an account you will be asked to submit your resume and answer additional relevant questions. Make sure you continue all the way through the application in addition to the initial profile information.

The online application is the place to put your best foot forward to the recruiting team and hiring manager. Be sure to complete all questions as thoroughly as possible.

The Interview

Our recruiters work closely with the hiring managers to assess applications for each job opening. If your application is selected, you will be contacted by a recruiter to schedule an initial interview. Typically the first interview is by phone. After that you will be contacted to inform you of next steps in the process. The process varies by business unit but typically includes in-person interviews with the hiring manager and other members of the team you would be working with.

To prepare for your interview, be sure to learn in advance about our company and culture—you will find information throughout our Careers site to help you prepare. Also, follow us on LinkedIn and Twitter for even more insight and information.

The Offer

If you have successfully completed the process and are identified as the candidate we would like to hire, your recruiter will reach out to you to discuss the details of the offer. You will also receive an offer letter to confirm the offer details and provide you with additional information. The recruiter will work closely with you to make sure all your questions are answered.

Pre-Employment

All offers of employment extended by HCSC are contingent upon successful completion of all lawful pre-employment checks, including but not limited to, background checks and drug screening. You’ll receive information on completing these checks from the recruiting team and will need to act on them in a timely manner.

Onboarding

Congratulations! Prior to your start date, you’ll receive information about HCSC via on our online onboarding system. Please read through the information and complete any requested tasks. We look forward to seeing you on your first day at HCSC!
