Social Responsibility Report

The core values of Health Care Service Corporation (HCSC) are reflected in our social responsibility commitments across a number of areas including corporate giving, local community relations, volunteerism, diversity and inclusion, ethics and compliance and sustainability initiatives. As the country’s largest customer-owned health insurer, we are dedicated to contributing to the well-being of the communities in which we live, work and play. We invite you to learn more about the progress we are making to positively impact the health and wellness of the individuals within our communities.
